☰ 乾 Qian – Heaven

← Back to Trigrams

Description

Qian represents heaven, creativity, and strong yang energy. It symbolizes strength, initiative, and the power of creation. It is pure, active, and unyielding, embodying leadership, ambition, and clarity of purpose.

Symbolism

  • Element: Heaven / Sky
  • Direction: Northwest
  • Family: Father
  • Attributes: Strong, creative, ambitious

Guidance

Qian encourages you to take decisive action, lead with integrity, and use your creative energy to initiate new projects or ideas. Focus, discipline, and perseverance are key.
