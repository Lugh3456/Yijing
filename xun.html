☴ 巽 Xun – Wind

← Back to Trigrams

Description

Xun represents wind, gentle penetration, and flexibility. It symbolizes adaptability, subtle influence, and the spread of energy. It encourages patience, diplomacy, and quiet guidance.

Symbolism

  • Element: Wind / Wood
  • Direction: Southeast
  • Family: Eldest Daughter
  • Attributes: Gentle, pervasive, adaptable

Guidance

Xun advises you to move subtly, influence carefully, and communicate effectively. Gentle persistence and adaptability lead to success rather than forceful action.
