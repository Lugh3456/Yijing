☷ 坤 Kun – Earth

← Back to Trigrams

Description

Kun represents the earth, receptivity, and yielding energy. It symbolizes nurturing, support, and pure yin energy. It encourages patience, cooperation, and openness to guidance.

Symbolism

  • Element: Earth
  • Direction: Southwest
  • Family: Mother
  • Attributes: Receptive, nurturing, flexible

Guidance

Kun advises you to follow, support, and nurture rather than lead. Success comes through patience, adaptability, and working harmoniously with others. Be receptive to opportunities and guidance from more experienced influences.
