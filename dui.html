☱ Dui – Lake

← Back to Trigrams

Description

Dui represents the lake or marsh, joyfulness, and openness. It symbolizes communication, receptivity, and harmony. It encourages playfulness, optimism, and social engagement.

Symbolism

  • Element: Lake / Marsh
  • Direction: West
  • Family: Youngest Daughter
  • Attributes: Joyful, open, communicative

Guidance

Dui advises openness, cooperation, and positivity. Success comes from creating harmony, sharing ideas, and embracing joyful collaboration.
