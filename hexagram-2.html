2. 坤 – Kūn (The Receptive)

← Back to Hexagrams

Trigrams

☷☷ – Earth above, Earth below

Meaning

Represents receptivity, nurturing, and yielding. Symbolizes earth and pure yin energy. Encourages patience, support, and cooperation.

Interpretation

  • Focus on following, supporting, and nurturing rather than leading.
  • Practice patience and openness to guidance.
  • Adaptability and humility are rewarded.

Six Yao (Lines) with Classical Quotes

1st Yao (初爻)

履霜，坚冰至。

English: Treading on frost. Hard ice will come. Be cautious and prepare carefully at the beginning.

Luck Level: Moderate

2nd Yao (二爻)

直方大，不习无不利。

English: Upright and square. Without error, all is beneficial. Success comes through honesty and diligence.

Luck Level: Good

3rd Yao (三爻)

含章可贞。或从王事，无成有终。

English: Containing brilliance. Persevere in integrity. Join in important affairs carefully; although progress is slow, the outcome is favorable.

Luck Level: Moderate

4th Yao (四爻)

括囊；无咎无誉。

English: Carrying a pouch. No blame, no praise. Stay cautious, remain humble, and avoid taking unnecessary risks.

Luck Level: Moderate

5th Yao (五爻)

黄裳元吉。

English: Yellow garment. Great fortune. Peak of receptivity and harmony; recognition and blessings come naturally.

Luck Level: Excellent

6th Yao (上爻)

龙战于野，其血玄黄。

English: Dragons fight in the field; their blood is dark yellow. Completion brings challenges and caution; avoid arrogance at the top.

Luck Level: Moderate
