Description

Li represents fire, light, and clarity. It symbolizes illumination, awareness, and guidance. It encourages knowledge, focus, and insight.

Symbolism

  • Element: Fire
  • Direction: South
  • Family: Middle Daughter
  • Attributes: Bright, clarifying, perceptive

Guidance

Li advises bringing clarity and understanding to situations. Use insight and wisdom to illuminate challenges and guide others effectively.
