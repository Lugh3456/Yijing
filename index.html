About Bagua Hexagrams

About Yijing and Bagua

The Yijing (I Ching) is an ancient Chinese text used for divination, philosophy, and guidance. The Bagua represents the 8 trigrams that form all 64 hexagrams, each with its own meaning and symbolism.

The 8 Trigrams (Bagua)

☰ Qian - Heaven

Creative, strong, forceful

☱ Dui - Lake

Joyful, open, receptive

☲ Li - Fire

Clinging, light, clarity

☳ Zhen - Thunder

Movement, initiative, arousal

☴ Xun - Wind

Pervasive, gentle, penetration

☵ Kan - Water

Danger, depth, flow

☶ Gen - Mountain

Stillness, stability, obstruction

☷ Kun - Earth

Receptive, yielding, nurturing

The 64 Hexagrams

1. 乾 Qián – The Creative

Trigrams: ☰☰

Represents creativity, strength, and initiative. Symbolizes heaven and pure yang energy.

2. 坤 Kūn – The Receptive

Trigrams: ☷☷

Represents receptivity, nurturing, and yielding. Symbolizes earth and pure yin energy.

3. 屯 Zhun – Difficulty at the Beginning

Trigrams: ☰☷

Represents growth from confusion and challenge. Encourages perseverance at the start of a new venture.

4. 蒙 Meng – Youthful Folly

Trigrams: ☷☰

Represents learning through inexperience. Advises openness and humility to gain wisdom.

5. 需 Xu – Waiting (Nourishment)

Trigrams: ☰☵

Represents patience and proper timing. Suggests preparation before taking action.

6. 讼 Song – Conflict

Trigrams: ☵☰

Represents disputes or opposition. Encourages clear communication and fairness to resolve issues.

7. 师 Shi – The Army

Trigrams: ☰☷

Represents discipline, leadership, and collective effort. Success comes through organization and unity.

8. 比 Bi – Holding Together

Trigrams: ☷☰

Represents cooperation, loyalty, and community. Working together brings strength and stability.

9. 小畜 Xiao Xu – Taming the Power of the Small

Trigrams: ☰☴

Represents small progress and gentle restraint. Encourages careful, steady efforts to achieve goals.

10. 履 Lü – Treading (Conduct)

Trigrams: ☷☰

Represents careful behavior and correct conduct. Advises mindfulness and respect when moving forward.

11. 泰 Tai – Peace

Trigrams: ☷☰

Represents harmony, balance, and smooth progress. A favorable time where obstacles are minimal.

12. 否 Pi – Standstill

Trigrams: ☰☷

Represents stagnation and obstacles. Advises patience and maintaining integrity during difficult times.

13. 同人 Tong Ren – Fellowship with Men

Trigrams: ☰☱

Represents cooperation and shared purpose. Unity and harmonious relationships are emphasized.

14. 大有 Da You – Possession in Great Measure

Trigrams: ☱☰

Represents abundance, success, and influence. Use resources wisely and with generosity.

15. 谦 Qian – Modesty

Trigrams: ☷☶

Represents humility and self-restraint. True strength comes from modesty and patience.

16. 豫 Yu – Enthusiasm

Trigrams: ☱☰

Represents inspiration, motivation, and joyful action. Positive energy encourages progress.

17. 随 Sui – Following

Trigrams: ☱☰

Represents adaptability and going with the flow. Success comes by aligning with the right influences.

18. 蛊 Gu – Work on Decay

Trigrams: ☶☷

Represents reform, cleansing, and repairing what has gone wrong. Action is required to restore order.

19. 临 Lin – Approach

Trigrams: ☷☱

Represents guidance, influence, and favorable progress. Approach others with respect and support.

20. 观 Guan – Contemplation

Trigrams: ☶☱

Represents observation, reflection, and awareness. Understanding comes through careful contemplation.
