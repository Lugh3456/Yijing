Description

Kan represents water, danger, and depth. It symbolizes challenges, emotions, and hidden potential. It encourages caution, perseverance, and insight when facing uncertainty.

Symbolism

  • Element: Water
  • Direction: North
  • Family: Middle Son
  • Attributes: Deep, fluid, cautious

Guidance

Kan advises careful planning and awareness of risks. Success comes from understanding the depth of situations, navigating challenges wisely, and remaining adaptable.
