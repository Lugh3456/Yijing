1. 乾 – Qián (The Creative)

← Back to Hexagrams

Trigrams

☰☰ – Heaven above, Heaven below

Meaning

Represents creativity, strength, and initiative. Symbolizes heaven and pure yang energy. Encourages leadership, confidence, and the beginning of great projects.

Interpretation

  • Focus on action and initiative.
  • Be bold but wise in leadership.
  • Maintain clarity, discipline, and perseverance.

Six Yao (Lines) with Classical Quotes

1st Yao (初爻)

潜龙，勿用。

English: The hidden dragon. Do not act yet. Patience is required at the beginning.

Luck Level: Good

2nd Yao (二爻)

见龙在田，利见大人。

English: The dragon appears in the field. It is favorable to meet a great person. Progress is steady with guidance.

Luck Level: Very Good

3rd Yao (三爻)

君子终日乾乾，夕惕若厉，无咎。

English: The noble one works diligently all day, vigilant at night. No blame. Challenges may appear; persistence brings success.

Luck Level: Moderate

4th Yao (四爻)

或跃在渊，无咎。

English: Leaping into the abyss. No blame. Leadership is tested; careful planning needed.

Luck Level: Good

5th Yao (五爻)

飞龙在天，利见大人。

English: The flying dragon in the heavens. It is favorable to meet a great person. Peak of strength and good fortune.

Luck Level: Excellent

6th Yao (上爻)

亢龙，有悔。

English: The dragon is overreaching. There is regret. Completion and culmination; be cautious of overreach.

Luck Level: Moderate
