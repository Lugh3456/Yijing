☶ 艮 Gen – Mountain

← Back to Trigrams

Description

Gen represents mountain, stillness, and stability. It symbolizes introspection, patience, and endurance. It encourages reflection and careful consideration before action.

Symbolism

  • Element: Mountain / Earth
  • Direction: Northeast
  • Family: Youngest Son
  • Attributes: Still, stable, contemplative

Guidance

Gen advises patience, contemplation, and remaining grounded. Success comes from reflection, careful planning, and knowing when to act.
