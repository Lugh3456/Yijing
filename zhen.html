☳ 震 Zhen – Thunder

← Back to Trigrams

Description

Zhen represents thunder, movement, and initiative. It symbolizes action, energy, and the awakening of potential. It encourages courage and taking decisive steps forward.

Symbolism

  • Element: Thunder
  • Direction: East
  • Family: Eldest Son
  • Attributes: Energetic, initiating, bold

Guidance

Zhen advises you to embrace action and move forward with courage. Success comes from initiative and not hesitating when opportunity strikes. Use your energy wisely to create momentum.
